About Zeph

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to breathe better.

Our Story

Breathing Better, Together

Zeph was founded with a simple but powerful belief: respiratory health technology should be accessible to everyone — not just those who can make it to a clinic. We set out to build a platform that brings clinical-grade accuracy into the hands of everyday people.

Our mission is to democratize respiratory health. By combining medical-precision sensors with an intuitive app experience, Zeph empowers patients, clinicians, and families to monitor, train, and improve lung function from anywhere.

  • Founded on the belief that breathing health is a right, not a privilege
  • Committed to clinical-grade accuracy in a consumer-friendly form
  • Backed by leading respiratory researchers and healthcare institutions

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build at Zeph.

Science-Driven

Every exercise and recommendation in Zeph is backed by respiratory research. We partner with leading institutions and clinical researchers to ensure our platform delivers evidence-based results.

Patient-First

We design for real people with real needs. From seniors managing COPD to athletes pushing their limits, Zeph is intuitive, accessible, and built around the patient experience.

Clinical-Grade

Medical-precision technology in a consumer-friendly form. Our sensors deliver the same accuracy as clinical spirometry, giving you and your care team data you can trust.
