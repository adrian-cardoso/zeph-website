About Zeph

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to breathe better.

Our Story

With Zeph, it's very personal

Our founders saw both of their parents suffer from COPD. When their mother contracted COVID-19, it triggered a severe exacerbation. Witnessing the challenges of rehabilitation brought the need for better treatment options to light, and that's how Zeph was born.

Meet the Team

CMO

Shikha Anand, MD, MPH

Shikha is a practicing physician and the former Chief Medical Officer of Aetna/CVS and Withings. She is a seasoned former researcher and has experience scaling clinical applications and medical devices, focused on creating positive health behavior changes. Her innovative programs at Walmart have touched countless lives in the US.

CEO

Mimi Gendreau Kigawa

Mimi studied design engineering at Harvard, where she discovered her calling — improving how patients heal from home. She's poured her energy into crafting innovative inventions to help patients do just that. These include a pneumatic offloading shoe for neuropathic patients and a menstrual diagnostic tool to detect early signs of cervical cancer in menstruating women.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build at Zeph.

Science-Driven

Every exercise and recommendation in Zeph is backed by respiratory research. We partner with leading institutions and clinical researchers to ensure our platform delivers evidence-based results.

Patient-First

We design for real people with real needs. From seniors managing COPD to people with seasonal allergies, Zeph is intuitive, accessible, and built around the patient experience.

Clinical-Grade

Medical-precision technology in a consumer-friendly form. Our sensors deliver the same accuracy as clinical spirometry, giving you and your care team data you can trust.

We are building the future of respiratory health. Whether you are a patient, clinician, or partner, we would love to hear from you.
