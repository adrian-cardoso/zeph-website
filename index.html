Zeph is a clinical-grade smart breath trainer and telerehab platform designed to improve respiratory outcomes for chronic lung patients. It combines sensor-driven breathing exercises with an AI-powered clinician dashboard for remote patient monitoring.
Yes. Zeph sessions can be billed under existing CPT codes for pulmonary rehabilitation and remote patient monitoring. Our team can help you identify the appropriate billing codes for your practice and payer mix.
Zeph is designed for patients with chronic pulmonary conditions including COPD, asthma, and other respiratory diseases who would benefit from pulmonary rehabilitation. Ideal candidates include patients post-hospitalization, those with declining lung function, or patients who have difficulty accessing in-person rehab programs.
Zeph monitors key respiratory metrics in real-time during every session. Our clinical team reviews patient data regularly, and the platform alerts care teams to any concerning trends. All exercises are designed by respiratory specialists with built-in safety parameters to prevent overexertion.
We track lung function metrics (FEV1, FVC, PEF), patient compliance and adherence rates, quality of life scores, hospital readmission rates, and exercise tolerance. Our dashboard provides longitudinal data to demonstrate clinical improvement over the course of treatment.
Zeph is designed for simplicity. Patients receive a complete kit with easy setup instructions, and our onboarding team provides guided walkthroughs. The device works via Bluetooth with a tablet, and sessions can be completed offline and synced later. An internet connection is needed only for data syncing with the clinician dashboard.
Zeph supports integration with major EHR systems to streamline clinical workflows. Patient session data, compliance reports, and respiratory metrics can be exported directly into patient records, reducing manual documentation and keeping care teams informed within their existing systems.
To get started, simply request a demo and our partnerships team will guide you through implementation. Patients receive a Zeph kit shipped directly to their home, which includes the smart breath trainer, a tablet with the Zeph platform pre-loaded, and all necessary accessories.
Zeph provides structured, guided pulmonary rehabilitation that patients can complete from home. The personalized exercise plans, real-time feedback, and gamified experience drive higher adherence rates than traditional rehab programs, leading to measurable improvements in lung function and quality of life.
Our program incorporates guided breathing exercises that progressively build respiratory endurance and capacity. Sessions combine resistance breathing, paced breathing techniques, and active recovery cycles — designed by respiratory therapists to improve cardiopulmonary fitness safely and effectively.
