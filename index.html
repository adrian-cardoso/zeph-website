How It Works

Integrate Zeph Into Your Practice

Deploying Zeph with your patients is simple. Our platform fits into your existing workflow in three steps.

1

Prescribe Zeph

Identify patients who would benefit from respiratory training. Zeph ships directly to patients with everything they need to get started.

2

Patients Train at Home

Patients follow personalized breathing exercise plans guided by the app. Clinical-grade sensors capture spirometry-quality data with every session.

3

Monitor Remotely

Access your provider dashboard to track patient compliance, review respiratory trends, and make data-driven care decisions between visits.
